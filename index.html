Vision

Start with food. Build the network.

Hylander Mobile begins as a food truck brand with a practical path into late-night delivery, convenience runs, courier work, and local logistics across Hudson Valley towns.

People-first operations

The network is designed around local demand, better driver treatment, realistic service zones, and community relationships with restaurants, venues, shops, and residents.

Markets

Hudson Valley first. Regional scale next.

Kingston Rhinebeck Beacon Poughkeepsie Woodstock Hudson / Catskill Albany Westchester NYC
Project

A local commerce platform hiding inside a food truck.

The goal is to prove repeatable demand in one town, build reliable operating playbooks, then expand market-by-market into a regional late-night logistics network.
